GARITS ◂

The Website System (GARITS) was created for automobile mechanics and their businesses, allowing them to efficiently manage customer records, vehicle details, servicing jobs, parts inventory, and job invoices. When a vehicle is scheduled for servicing, GARITS generates a comprehensive job sheet for the mechanic, outlining the required tasks. Once the job is completed, the system calculates the total time spent and the cost of spare parts, generating an accurate invoice for the customer. The parts record keeps track of part quantities, automatically triggering reordering when stock levels are low. Throughout the system's design process, user-friendliness was prioritized, ensuring ease of use for all users. Additionally, robustness, efficiency, and security were key considerations, ensuring a reliable and effective system.
